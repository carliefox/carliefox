How To Help Native Wisconsin Species

A win-win for you and the birds

Oak trees (Quercus species) support over 530 species of butterflies and moth caterpillars, more than any other woody plant.Many migrating and nesting birds rely on these caterpillars for food, including such favorites as warblers, tanagers, and orioles. Acorns are eaten by blue jays, red-headed woodpeckers and many other native species. The best thing you can do is not cut down the trees!
